About Gilivindu

Gilivindu is a residential summer camp created to give children a joyful, meaningful, and enriching holiday experience.

Gilivindu is a residential summer camp specially designed for children between 8 to 13 years.

Started with a small dream and a lot of love for children, Gilivindu is now proudly entering its 5th year. Over the years, many children have been part of this journey ‚Äî learning, playing, growing, and forming friendships that last beyond the camp.

Gilivindu is not just a camp. It is a space where children slow down, connect with nature, discover themselves, and build life skills in a safe and nurturing environment.

A message from the founder

Gilivindu was born from a simple and deeply personal thought ‚Äî what kind of childhood do children truly deserve? Growing up, I always wished for a space that was not rushed, not judged, and not over-scheduled, but one where I could simply be myself. A place filled with curiosity, laughter, friendships, nature, and the freedom to explore without fear. I could not find that space as a child, so I decided to create it for others. Gilivindu is my way of offering children the kind of childhood I once longed for ‚Äî a space to slow down, to feel safe, to discover who they are, and to grow with joy. Every year, when I see children leave with shining eyes and parents share their heartfelt gratitude, I am reminded why this journey began and why it continues, year after year.

Vibha K R
Professional Educator, Lifelong Learner

At Gilivindu, we believe that:

  • Children learn best when they feel safe, respected, and joyful.
  • Real learning happens through experience, not pressure.
  • Every child is unique and needs space to grow at their own pace.
  • Childhood should include play, creativity, silence, laughter, mistakes, and reflection.

We aim to create an environment where children:

  • feel heard,
  • feel valued,
  • and feel free to be themselves.

At Gilivindu, we focus on the overall growth of the child ‚Äî emotional, social, physical, and creative.

Through thoughtfully designed activities and daily experiences, children get opportunities to:

  • communicate with confidence
  • work together in groups
  • understand and manage their emotions
  • become more independent
  • develop respect for nature and people

Learning at Gilivindu happens naturally and joyfully. There is no pressure to perform ‚Äî only gentle guidance, encouragement, and space for each child to grow at their own pace.

Gilivindu is an 8-day residential camp.

Children stay at the campus, away from gadgets, school pressure, and routine schedules.

This residential setting helps them:

  • build independence,
  • develop responsibility,
  • learn self-care,
  • and form deep friendships.

Each day is balanced with:

  • physical activities,
  • creative sessions,
  • quiet reflection,
  • group discussions,
  • and free play.

Our activities are carefully designed to nurture mind, body, heart, and imagination.

We follow a non-authoritarian, child-friendly approach.

Facilitators:

  • listen, not lecture
  • encourage, not compare

We focus on:

  • positive reinforcement
  • emotional safety
  • respectful discipline
  • gentle boundaries
Every child is treated as an individual, not a number.

Your child‚Äôs safety is our highest priority.
